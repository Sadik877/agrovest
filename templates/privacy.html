Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Introduction

AgroVest Pro ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA). This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over it.

2. Information We Collect

  • Identity data: full name, email address, phone number, and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Financial data: deposit and withdrawal bank account details, transaction history, balance, and investment records.
  • Verification data: proof-of-payment documents or images you upload for deposit verification.
  • Usage data: log-in timestamps, device/browser information, and IP address, collected automatically.
  • Referral data: your referral code and information linking referred accounts to yours.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and administer your account and process deposits, investments, and withdrawals;
  • To calculate and credit daily profit and referral commissions;
  • To verify your identity and payments, and to detect and prevent fraud;
  • To send you transactional notifications (deposit confirmations, profit credits, security alerts);
  • To respond to support requests and comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the basis of: performance of the contract between you and AgroVest Pro (managing your account and investments); our legitimate interests (fraud prevention, platform security); and compliance with applicable legal obligations (e.g. anti-money-laundering checks).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Infrastructure and hosting providers (e.g. our database and application hosting providers) strictly to operate the Platform;
  • Payment and banking partners, to process deposits and withdrawals;
  • Regulators or law enforcement, where required by law or a valid legal request.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active, and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal, tax, and anti-fraud recordkeeping obligations. You may request deletion of your data subject to these legal retention requirements.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including password hashing, encrypted connections (HTTPS), and access controls on our systems. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

8. Your Rights

Under the NDPA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations;
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data;
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [privacy@agrovest.ng].

9. Cookies

We use essential session cookies required for you to log in and use the Platform securely. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the Platform or by email before they take effect.
