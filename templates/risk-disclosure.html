Risk Disclosure

Last updated: July 3, 2026. All investors must read and understand this disclosure before committing funds to any AgroVest Pro plan.

Agricultural investment carries real financial risk, including the possible loss of some or all of your principal. Projected returns shown on AgroVest Pro are targets, not guarantees. Do not invest funds you cannot afford to have locked up or, in a worst case, lose entirely.

1. Nature of Agricultural Risk

Returns on AgroVest Pro plans are generated from underlying farming and agribusiness activity. That activity is inherently exposed to factors outside our control, including but not limited to:

  • Adverse weather, drought, flooding, and seasonal variability
  • Crop disease, pest infestation, and livestock health events
  • Commodity price fluctuations and market demand shifts
  • Supply chain, logistics, and input cost disruptions
  • Regulatory, land tenure, or policy changes affecting Nigerian agriculture

2. Returns Are Not Guaranteed

The ROI percentage, daily return, and total return figures displayed for each plan are projections based on expected farm performance. They are targets, not contractual guarantees. Past performance of any plan — including any historical payouts shown on this platform — is not a reliable indicator of future results. In an adverse scenario, actual returns may be lower than projected, delayed, or in extreme cases, principal may not be fully recoverable.

3. Liquidity & Lock-in Risk

Funds committed to a plan are generally locked for that plan's stated duration. AgroVest Pro is not a savings account or a demand-deposit product — you should not invest funds you may need to access on short notice. Where an early-withdrawal option is offered on a specific plan, it may be subject to fees, reduced returns, or processing delays, as described on that plan's terms.

4. Platform & Counterparty Risk

As with any investment platform, there is a risk associated with the platform operator itself — including operational, technological, and counterparty risk. We take reasonable steps to safeguard user funds and data, but no platform can eliminate this category of risk entirely.

5. No Investment Advice

Nothing on AgroVest Pro constitutes financial, legal, or tax advice. Plan descriptions, marketing materials, and projected figures are provided for informational purposes only. You are solely responsible for evaluating whether a given plan is suitable for your financial circumstances, and we encourage you to consult an independent licensed financial advisor before investing.

6. Diversification

We recommend not concentrating all of your investable funds into a single plan or platform. Spreading risk across multiple asset types is a widely accepted principle of prudent personal finance.

7. Acknowledgement

By creating an account and activating an investment plan on AgroVest Pro, you confirm that you have read, understood, and accepted this Risk Disclosure in full, and that you are investing funds you can reasonably afford to have locked for the plan duration and, in a worst case, to lose.
